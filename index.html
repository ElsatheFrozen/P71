Activities

Learning with fun’ is the concept of the modern education, keeping in view this predominant thought, around two years back, GAIL DAV public school institutionalized different clubs in its curriculum to hone the latent talent of every child in all the spheres of life. The introduction of the clubs under mentioned has opened a wide range of vistas for the children to exhibit their different skills. 1. Science 2. Environment 3. Literary 4. Computer 5. Art and Craft 6. Dance and music 7. Health and wellness 8. Home science All the above mentioned clubs are committed to bring out the inspiring inherent versatility of the children by designing the different kind of activities which will definitely build a comprehensive understanding in a child’s approach.
